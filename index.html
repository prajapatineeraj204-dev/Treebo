Our Story

Welcome to Hotel, where our passion for hospitality meets luxury.

Founded in 2010 by partners Alex Johnson and Maria Rodriguez, Hotel began as a dream to create a sanctuary for travelers seeking both comfort and elegance. What started as a small 10-room boutique hotel has now grown into a 50-room luxury establishment, renowned for its exceptional service and attention to detail.

Our journey began when Alex and Maria, both seasoned hospitality professionals, decided to combine their expertise to create a unique hotel experience. They envisioned a place where guests would feel at home while enjoying premium amenities and personalized service.

Over the years, Grand Vista has received numerous awards including "Best Luxury Hotel 2022" and "Excellence in Hospitality 2021". Our commitment to sustainability and community engagement has been recognized with the "Green Hotel Certification" for three consecutive years.

Today, we continue to evolve while staying true to our founding principles: exceptional service, attention to detail, and creating memorable experiences for every guest.
